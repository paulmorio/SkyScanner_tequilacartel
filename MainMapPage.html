DEPARTURE DATES

In order to operate please choose a starting airport, the default is Edinburgh. Then choose date or set of dates using the familiar shift- and control- click keys. the duration of the trip can also be chosen at this point. Choosing a date will color the map, in different hues depending on the price of the flight including its return flight. Hovering the mouse pointer over a country will then display this price. It is also possible to move around and click on different countries in order to zoom in to this particular country.
